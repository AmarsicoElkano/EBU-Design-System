Public service media is at the beating heart of our democracy, our society, our culture and our economy. It delivers vital news and information, provides opportunities for both education and entertainment, supports the creative industries, celebrates cultural identity and builds shared national and European values. It helps us understand who we are and our place in this increasingly complex, fragmented world. It is an essential component of a well-functioning democracy and a vibrant, inclusive society.

STEP 1

The strategy approach

A solid and logical methodology should always be used to define the cornerstones of a national PSM advocacy programme, with concrete communications and engagement measures. Defining the overall objective is the start point of all strategic considerations, to ensure PSM representatives, who will engage with local stakeholders, know and understand the “why” and “how” of targeted engagement concepts.
